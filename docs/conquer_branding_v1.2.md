**CONQUER CARD**

Branding & Go-to-Market Brief

Version 1.2 — Global positioning

# **1\. Positioning Statement**

Conquer Card is a social card game built for people who take cards seriously. It is not positioned as a regional or cultural product — it is a global product with deep roots that inform its design without limiting its audience.

***"Strategic melding. Real stakes. Play with anyone, anywhere. Feel the table."***

The game's mechanics speak for themselves: a double deck, a 41-point opening threshold, Joker finish for double coins, the secret Face-Up Card strategy, the Perfect Hand. These are compelling to any card game player in the world. Lead with the mechanics, not the origin.

# **4\. Go-to-Market Strategy**

## **4.1 Global First, Community Layer Second**

The product launches globally with universal positioning. In parallel, a targeted community marketing layer runs separately — reaching specific communities who already know and love this game. The product does not carry cultural labels in the app itself, but marketing communications can be deeply targeted.

| **Audience** | **How to reach them** |
| --- | --- |
| Card game enthusiasts (global) | App Store search optimization, card game communities on Reddit and Discord |
| Diaspora communities | Targeted TikTok and Facebook content in their language — 'the game you grew up playing is now on your phone' |
| Rummy players | The mechanics are similar enough that rummy players will immediately understand and enjoy Conquer Card |
| Social gamers | Pokerface-style positioning — emphasize the live social layer over the card mechanics |
| Friends and family networks | Private table invite is the strongest growth mechanic — design for sharing |

## **4.2 Pre-Launch Actions**

1.  Post in card game communities online: 'I built a strategic melding card game with live voice — who wants to beta test?'
2.  Create a waitlist landing page — one page, email signup, 30-second demo video
3.  Start a TikTok account @conquercardapp — 'I built a card game for mobile' is a compelling story universally
4.  Find 10 beta testers from communities who know the game — get honest feedback before launch
5.  Create a private Discord or Telegram for beta testers — this becomes your core community

## **4.3 Community Marketing Layer (runs separately)**

This is the targeted layer that runs on top of the global positioning. It is not part of the app store listing — it is content and community marketing:

-   TikTok content in multiple languages showing the game being played in family settings
-   Facebook group posts targeted to diaspora communities who already know the game
-   'The card game you grew up playing — now on your phone' messaging in community channels
-   Amharic UI added in v1.5 — announced specifically to that community as a feature
-   Special themed table releasing as a community milestone reward

## **4.4 Referral Mechanic**

-   Private table room code — every share is a direct referral with immediate activation
-   One tap to copy room code, one tap to share via WhatsApp
-   v1.5: referral bonus — invite a friend who completes their first game, both get 50 coins
